ABOUT

INNATA Foundation is a Social Enterprise Organization. Our mission is to bring advanced 21st century technology to Patients with conditions underlying cause is genetics, and connect them to advanced medicine based on precision medicine treatments.

Mission

Our mission is to bring advanced 21st century technology to Patients with genetic underlying conditions build the bridges to its Healthcare ecosystem.

Vision

  • Help to bring access to any patient suffering genetic diseases to advanced medicine regardless on where they are.
  • Building an inclusive platform using advanced medicine and Technology.
